Research Methodology Dashboard

Distributional Cues in Construction Acquisition: A Comparative Study of Native and Nonnative English Speakers

Participants研究参与者
67
26 native English speakers and 41 non-native English speakers were recruited from an Australian university. Non-native speakers had taken standardized proficiency tests (IELTS, PTE, TOEFL) with mean IELTS-converted score of 7.19.
从澳大利亚一所大学招募了26名英语母语者和41名非英语母语者。 非英语母语者已参加标准化英语水平测试(雅思、PTE、托福)，平均换算后的雅思分数为7.19。
Study Design研究设计
2
The study comprised two experiments:
• Experiment 1: Sentence completion task
• Experiment 2: Verb retrieval task
Both experiments focused on the as-predicative construction.
该研究包含两个实验：
• 实验1：句子完成任务
• 实验2：动词检索任务
两个实验都聚焦于as-谓语结构。
Variables变量
5
Five key variables were examined:
• Verb cue validity (ΔP(construction|verb))
• Construction cue validity (ΔP(verb|construction))
• Verb frequency
• Voice (active/passive)
• L2 proficiency (for non-native speakers)
研究考察了五个关键变量：
• 动词提示有效性 (ΔP(construction|verb))
• 结构提示有效性 (ΔP(verb|construction))
• 动词频率
• 语态（主动/被动）
• 二语水平（针对非英语母语者）
Experimental Procedure
实验程序
Corpus Analysis Preparation
语料库分析准备
The British National Corpus (BNC) was used to calculate ΔP indices for verbs occurring in the as-predicative construction. This data informed the selection of stimuli.
使用英国国家语料库(BNC)计算了as-谓语结构中出现的动词的ΔP指数。这些数据为刺激选择提供了依据。
Experiment 1: Sentence Completion
实验1：句子完成
Participants completed sentence fragments preceded by contextual prompts. Each fragment consisted of an NP subject and a verb cue. 48 target stimuli and 36 fillers were created, split into two test versions according to voice distinction.
参与者完成了有上下文提示的句子片段。每个片段包含一个名词短语主语和一个动词提示。研究者创建了48个目标刺激和36个填充刺激，根据语态区分分为两个测试版本。
Experiment 2: Verb Retrieval
实验2：动词检索
Participants supplied verbs to complete sentences cued with the as-predicative construction. 12 target stimuli and 12 fillers were created, with fragments in active or passive variants.
参与者提供动词以完成带有as-谓语结构提示的句子。研究者创建了12个目标刺激和12个填充刺激，句子片段有主动或被动语态变体。
Data Collection
数据收集
Experiment 1 used Psytoolkit online platform, while Experiment 2 used Qualtrics. Average duration was 20 minutes for each experiment.
实验1使用了Psytoolkit在线平台，而实验2使用了Qualtrics。每个实验的平均持续时间为20分钟。
Statistical Analysis Methods
统计分析方法
Bayesian Approach
贝叶斯方法
The study used Bayesian statistical analysis instead of p-value-driven hypothesis testing. This allowed for direct inferences based on data and observation of probability distributions.
本研究使用贝叶斯统计分析而非基于p值的假设检验。这允许直接基于数据进行推断并观察概率分布。
Logistic Regression (Exp. 1)
逻辑回归（实验1）
Bayesian mixed-effects logistic regression was used in Experiment 1, with binary outcome coding for presence/absence of as-predicative construction.
实验1使用了贝叶斯混合效应逻辑回归，以二元结果编码as-谓语结构的存在/缺失。
Negative Binomial Regression (Exp. 2)
负二项回归（实验2）
Bayesian mixed-effects negative binomial regression was used in Experiment 2 for count data analysis of verb retrievals.
实验2使用了贝叶斯混合效应负二项回归，用于动词检索的计数数据分析。
ROPE Analysis
实用等价区间分析
Region of Practical Equivalence (ROPE) was used to determine whether parameter values were equivalent to zero, different from zero, or uncertain.
使用实用等价区间(ROPE)来确定参数值是否等同于零、不同于零或不确定。
Fixed & Random Effects
固定和随机效应
Models included fixed effects for speaker, voice, verb cue validity, construction cue validity, and verb frequency, with random effects for items and participants.
模型包括说话者、语态、动词提示有效性、结构提示有效性和动词频率的固定效应，以及项目和参与者的随机效应。
Software
软件
Analysis was performed using R with the brms package (v2.20.4) for Bayesian regression, emmeans for interactions, and bayestR for ROPE calculations.
分析使用R语言，通过brms包(v2.20.4)进行贝叶斯回归，使用emmeans分析交互作用，并使用bayestR计算ROPE。
Key Methodological Quotes
关键方法论引述
"We adopt a Bayesian perspective for several reasons. Firstly, it allows us to directly and more accurately draw inferences based on our data. That is, we shift perspective from asking 'Given the null hypothesis, how probable is our data?' to asking 'Given our data, how probable is the null hypothesis?'"

"我们采用贝叶斯视角有几个原因。首先，它允许我们基于数据直接且更准确地进行推断。也就是说，我们转变视角，从问'在零假设条件下，我们的数据有多大可能性？'到问'基于我们的数据，零假设有多大可能性？'"

"We define a region of practical equivalence (ROPE) around the uncertainty of the true parameter value known as the highest density interval (HDI). The ROPE is ascertained as the range of values in the posterior distribution around the null value that contains a span of parameter values corresponding to 'practically no effect'."
Page 13
"我们围绕真实参数值的不确定性定义了一个实用等价区间(ROPE)，即所谓的最高密度区间(HDI)。ROPE被确定为后验分布中零值周围的一系列值，其中包含对应于'实际上没有效应'的参数值范围。"
第13页
"In Experiment 1, we observed that both speaker groups incorporated verb cue validity and construction cue validity in their construction retrieval, when cued by a verb. This suggests that speakers took into account both the verb's predictive validity and its category membership, with the former having a slightly stronger influence."

"在实验1中，我们观察到当由动词提示时，两组说话者都在其结构检索中整合了动词提示有效性和结构提示有效性。这表明说话者同时考虑了动词的预测有效性和其类别成员资格，前者影响略强。"
