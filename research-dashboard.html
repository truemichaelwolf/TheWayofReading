Research Overview

研究概述

This dashboard visualizes key findings from the research study "Distributional Cues in Construction Acquisition: A Comparative Study of Native and Nonnative English Speakers Using the As-Predicative Construction" by Ivana Domazetoska and Helen Zhao (2025).

本仪表板可视化了Ivana Domazetoska和Helen Zhao（2025年）的研究"构式习得中的分布线索：以as谓语构式为例的英语母语者和非母语者对比研究"的关键发现。

"This study investigates how distributional cues are integrated into the mental representation of the as-predicative construction by English native and nonnative speakers, drawing on associative learning theory. We examined speakers' constructional retrieval when given a verbal cue (Experiment 1) and their verb retrieval when given a constructional cue (Experiment 2)."
"本研究基于联想学习理论，考察了英语母语者和非母语者如何将分布线索整合到as谓语构式的心理表征中。我们研究了说话者在给定动词线索时的构式提取（实验1）和在给定构式线索时的动词提取（实验2）。"

Key Concepts

关键概念

Verb Cue Validity

动词线索有效性

The predictive power of a verb to cue a constructional outcome, or ΔP (construction|verb). This reflects how reliably a verb predicts the occurrence of a specific construction.

动词预测构式结果的能力，即ΔP（构式|动词）。这反映了动词预测特定构式出现的可靠性。

"Verb cue validity stands for the predictive power of the verb to cue a constructional outcome, or ΔP (construction|verb)."
"动词线索有效性代表动词提示构式结果的预测能力，即ΔP（构式|动词）。"

Construction Cue Validity

构式线索有效性

The predictive strength of the construction to cue a verbal outcome, or ΔP (verb|construction). This reflects how reliably a construction predicts the occurrence of a specific verb.

构式预测动词结果的能力，即ΔP（动词|构式）。这反映了构式预测特定动词出现的可靠性。

"Construction cue validity (alternatively referred to as category validity) embodies the predictive strength of the construction to cue a verbal outcome in the other direction, or ΔP (verb|construction)."
"构式线索有效性（也称为类别有效性）体现了构式向另一方向预测动词结果的能力，即ΔP（动词|构式）。"

The As-Predicative Construction

As谓语构式

A syntactic structure (e.g., "They saw the situation as a failure") used to ascribe an attribute to the direct object as a reflection of the subject's perception, classification, or judgment.

一种句法结构（例如，"They saw the situation as a failure"），用于将属性赋予直接宾语，反映主语的感知、分类或判断。

"The as-predicative construction is used to ascribe an attribute to the direct object, as a reflection of the subject's perception, classification, or judgment."
"as谓语构式用于将属性赋予直接宾语，作为主语感知、分类或判断的反映。"

Associative Learning Theory

联想学习理论

The theory that language acquisition involves generating predictions about events based on their associations with other events, where one event serves as a cue to predict another event as an outcome.

该理论认为语言习得涉及根据事件与其他事件的关联来生成对事件的预测，其中一个事件作为线索来预测另一个事件作为结果。

"This study is underpinned by the premise that statistical learning involves generating predictions about events based on their associations with other events. This process entails tracking relationships between the events, where one event serves as a cue to predict the occurrence of another event as an outcome, which forms the basis of associative learning theory."
"本研究基于这样一个前提：统计学习涉及根据事件与其他事件的关联来生成对事件的预测。这一过程需要追踪事件之间的关系，其中一个事件作为线索来预测另一个事件作为结果的发生，这构成了联想学习理论的基础。"

Experiment 1: Construction Retrieval

实验1：构式提取

In Experiment 1, participants were given a verb cue and asked to complete a sentence. The researchers examined how verb cue validity and construction cue validity affected speakers' retrieval of the as-predicative construction.

在实验1中，参与者获得一个动词线索，并被要求完成一个句子。研究人员考察了动词线索有效性和构式线索有效性如何影响说话者对as谓语构式的提取。

Key Findings from Experiment 1

实验1的主要发现

Native Speakers

母语者

Integrated both verb and construction cue validity with a slight preference for construction cue validity

整合了动词和构式线索有效性，对构式线索有效性略有偏好

VS

非母语者

Showed stronger sensitivity to verb cue validity compared to construction cue validity

Factors Affecting Construction Retrieval

影响构式提取的因素

Verb Frequency
动词频率

Strong negative effect: Higher frequency verbs decreased the likelihood of as-predicative retrieval

强烈的负面影响：高频动词降低了as谓语构式提取的可能性

Voice (Active vs. Passive)
语态（主动vs.被动）

Significant effect: The as-predicative was more likely to be supplied in passive contexts

显著影响：在被动语境中，as谓语构式更有可能被提供

L2 Proficiency (for Nonnative Speakers)
二语熟练度（对于非母语者）

Positive effect: Higher proficiency increased likelihood of target structure retrieval, particularly with verb cue validity

积极影响：更高的熟练度增加了目标结构提取的可能性，特别是对于动词线索有效性

"In Experiment 1, we observed that both speaker groups incorporated verb cue validity and construction cue validity in their construction retrieval, when cued by a verb."
"在实验1中，我们观察到两组说话者在由动词提示的构式提取中都整合了动词线索有效性和构式线索有效性。"

Experiment 2: Verb Retrieval

实验2：动词提取

In Experiment 2, participants were given the as-predicative construction and asked to supply a verb. The researchers examined how verb cue validity and construction cue validity affected speakers' verb retrieval.

在实验2中，参与者获得as谓语构式，并被要求提供一个动词。研究人员考察了动词线索有效性和构式线索有效性如何影响说话者的动词提取。

Research Implications

研究启示

This research has important implications for understanding language acquisition and processing, particularly how distributional cues affect linguistic information retrieval.

这项研究对理解语言习得和处理具有重要意义，特别是分布线索如何影响语言信息提取。

Directional Asymmetry

方向不对称性

The research suggests an asymmetry in how distributional cues are integrated based on the direction of retrieval (forward vs. backward).

研究表明，基于提取方向（正向vs.反向），分布线索的整合存在不对称性。

"These findings raise a question regarding speakers' ability to integrate multiple distributional cues in parallel and the possible role of directionality in the retrieval of linguistic information."
"这些发现引发了一个关于说话者平行整合多个分布线索的能力以及方向性在语言信息提取中可能作用的问题。"

Native vs. Nonnative Processing

母语者vs.非母语者处理

The study reveals differences in how native and nonnative speakers integrate distributional cues, with L2 proficiency playing a significant role.

研究揭示了母语者和非母语者整合分布线索的差异，二语熟练度起着重要作用。

"The present study supports the view that constructional categories partially emerge as a result of the integration of distributional information hinging on normative ΔP theory."
"本研究支持这一观点：构式类别部分是由基于规范性ΔP理论的分布信息整合而产生的。"

Cognitive Mechanisms

认知机制

Different cognitive mechanisms may be involved in construction retrieval (prediction generation) versus verb retrieval (memory search).

构式提取（预测生成）与动词提取（记忆搜索）可能涉及不同的认知机制。

"Integrating multiple distributional cues may be easier in a forward-retrieval process, which follows natural adjacency patterns found in the linguistic environment such as 'verb + as' (seen as, known as, regarded as, etc.), as opposed to a backward-retrieval process where a verb needs to be recalled within a given constructional context."
"在正向提取过程中整合多个分布线索可能更容易，这遵循语言环境中发现的自然邻接模式，如'动词 + as'（seen as, known as, regarded as等），而不是在给定构式语境中需要回忆动词的反向提取过程。"

Future Research Directions

未来研究方向

The researchers emphasize the need for more systematic research, particularly accounting for L2 proficiency and possible crosslinguistic influence.

研究人员强调需要更系统的研究，特别是考虑二语熟练度和可能的跨语言影响。

"We also strongly emphasize the need for more systematic research, particularly taking into account L2 proficiency and possible crosslinguistic influence."
"我们也强烈强调需要更系统的研究，特别是考虑到二语熟练度和可能的跨语言影响。"
