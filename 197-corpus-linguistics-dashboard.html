Key Findings

The paper highlights the rapid development of corpus linguistics in terms of practical and methodological perspectives, particularly in investigating language use in different contexts.

"The findings reveal a rapid development of the field in terms of practical and methodological perspectives, specifically regarding the investigations of language uses in different contexts. Thus, this paper indicates a significantly strong correlation between CL and technological development, such as natural language processing (NLP), and how this approach could fill the research gap of utilising CL in other areas of linguistics." - Altameemi (2024)

该论文强调了语料库语言学在实践和方法论视角方面的快速发展，特别是在研究不同语境下的语言使用方面。

"研究结果显示，该领域在实践和方法论方面有着快速发展，特别是关于在不同语境中语言使用的研究。因此，本文表明语料库语言学与技术发展（如自然语言处理（NLP））之间存在显著的强相关性，以及这种方法如何能够填补在语言学其他领域中使用语料库语言学的研究空白。" - Altameemi (2024)

Corpus Linguistics Tools

The paper provides a comprehensive review of various corpus linguistics tools, highlighting their features, advantages, and limitations.

AntConc

Pros: User-friendly, free, open source, multiple functions

Cons: Only processes plain text files, limited technical support

Sketch Engine

Pros: Large corpus (4B+ words), customizable queries, multiple languages

Cons: Paid service, slow loading, limited query types

LancsBox

Pros: Data visualization, collocation networks, multiple languages

Cons: Limited corpus size (max 50M words), limited support

"An important aspect that CL should consider is focusing on which tool facilitates answering the research question(s)." - Altameemi (2024)

该论文对各种语料库语言学工具进行了全面的评述，强调了它们的特点、优势和局限性。

AntConc

优点： 用户友好，免费，开源，多种功能

缺点： 只处理纯文本文件，技术支持有限

Sketch Engine

优点： 大型语料库（超过40亿词），可定制查询，多种语言

缺点： 付费服务，加载速度慢，查询类型有限

LancsBox

优点： 数据可视化，搭配网络，多种语言

缺点： 语料库大小有限（最大5000万词），技术支持有限

"语料库语言学应该考虑的一个重要方面是关注哪种工具有助于回答研究问题。" - Altameemi (2024)

Future of Corpus Linguistics with ChatGPT

The paper discusses the potential integration of corpus linguistics with ChatGPT and other NLP technologies:

  • ChatGPT can help in automatic building and annotating of corpora
  • Integration could reduce bias in corpus selection
  • ChatGPT might help identify linguistic patterns and errors
  • Potential to analyze large datasets more efficiently
"Since CL is dependent on the application of technological tools, it is important to discuss how the rapid development of technology influences the field of CL. Chat Generative Pre-trained Transformer (ChatGPT) is a search engine that utilises NLP. ChatGPT was selected for this research because it shares the notion with CL of focusing on applying the practical use of language in various contexts." - Altameemi (2024)

该论文讨论了语料库语言学与ChatGPT和其他自然语言处理技术的潜在整合：

  • ChatGPT可以帮助自动构建和注释语料库
  • 整合可以减少语料库选择中的偏见
  • ChatGPT可能有助于识别语言模式和错误
  • 有潜力更有效地分析大型数据集
"由于语料库语言学依赖于技术工具的应用，讨论技术的快速发展如何影响语料库语言学领域是很重要的。ChatGPT是一个利用自然语言处理的搜索引擎。选择ChatGPT进行此研究是因为它与语料库语言学有着共同的理念，即专注于在各种语境中应用语言的实际使用。" - Altameemi (2024)

Evolution of Corpus Linguistics

The evolution of corpus linguistics has been closely tied to technological advancements, from early computer-based corpora to modern NLP integration with tools like ChatGPT.

"With the evolvement of more sophisticated linguistic tools, researchers can delve even deeper into new research questions to investigate unexplored linguistic phenomena. Additionally, the integration of CL with ChatGPT holds great potential for the understanding of language in the digital age." - Altameemi (2024)

语料库语言学的演变与技术进步密切相关，从早期基于计算机的语料库到现代与ChatGPT等工具的自然语言处理集成。

"随着更复杂的语言学工具的发展，研究人员可以更深入地探索新的研究问题，研究未被探索的语言现象。此外，语料库语言学与ChatGPT的整合对于理解数字时代的语言具有巨大的潜力。" - Altameemi (2024)
