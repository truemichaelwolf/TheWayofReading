English-Medium Instruction (EMI) in European Higher Education

Overview & Key Findings

概述与主要发现

This dashboard presents data from a comprehensive study on English-medium instruction (EMI) in European higher education for the academic year 2023/2024, showing significant growth and changing patterns across Europe.

本仪表板展示了2023/2024学年欧洲高等教育中英语教学媒介(EMI)的综合研究数据，显示了整个欧洲显著的增长和变化模式。

"Notwithstanding the wide consensus that English-medium instruction (EMI) in European higher education has grown explosively since the turn of the century, there has been little research which addresses issues related to operationalizing EMI, nor has a pan-European update on EMI figures been given since 2013."

"尽管普遍认为自本世纪初以来，欧洲高等教育中的英语教学媒介(EMI)呈爆发式增长，但很少有研究解决与操作化EMI相关的问题，也没有自2013年以来对欧洲EMI数据进行全面更新。"

- Wingrove et al., 2025
- Wingrove等，2025年

Key Findings:

主要发现：

  • A total of 24,043 English-taught programs (ETPs) were identified in 2023/2024, showing approximately three-fold growth over the past decade from 8,089 in 2013.
  • EMI is now a pan-European phenomenon, no longer concentrated primarily in Northern Europe.
  • EMI is practiced primarily at the master's level, with 17,325 master's programs compared to 6,718 bachelor's programs.
  • The most popular disciplines are business and management (27.4%), engineering and technology (14.7%), and computer science and IT (10.6%).
  • 2023/2024学年共识别出24,043个英语教学项目(ETPs)，比2013年的8,089个项目增长了约三倍。
  • EMI如今已成为泛欧洲现象，不再主要集中在北欧地区。
  • EMI主要在硕士阶段实施，硕士项目17,325个，本科项目6,718个。
  • 最受欢迎的学科是商业与管理(27.4%)、工程与技术(14.7%)以及计算机科学与IT(10.6%)。

EMI Adoption by Region

The percentage of higher education institutions (HEIs) offering at least one English-taught program has increased across Europe, with significant regional variations:

提供至少一个英语教学项目的高等教育机构(HEIs)百分比在整个欧洲都有所增长，但地区差异显著：

Regional Highlights:

地区亮点：

  • Nordic Countries: Consistently high with approximately 60% of HEIs offering EMI, showing signs of saturation.
  • Central West Europe: Growth from 44.5% to 54.4%, with countries like the Netherlands leading at 75%.
  • Central East Europe: Significant growth from 19.9% to 46.9%, transitioning from low-EMI to medium-EMI status.
  • South West Europe: Explosive growth from 17.2% to 50.1%, with Spain showing 3.5-fold growth.
  • South East Europe: Grew from 18.3% to 31.5%, remaining the region with the lowest EMI adoption.
  • Baltic Countries: Modest growth from 38.7% to 43.2%, with high concentration of programs per student.
  • 北欧国家：持续保持高水平，约60%的高等教育机构提供EMI，显示饱和迹象。
  • 中西欧：从44.5%增长到54.4%，荷兰等国领先，达到75%。
  • 中东欧：显著增长，从19.9%增至46.9%，从低EMI状态转变为中等EMI状态。
  • 西南欧：爆发性增长，从17.2%增至50.1%，西班牙增长3.5倍。
  • 东南欧：从18.3%增长到31.5%，仍是EMI采用率最低的地区。
  • 波罗的海国家：适度增长，从38.7%至43.2%，但每名学生的项目集中度高。

"The north-south rift in EMI in European higher education appears to have evaporated. The divide, if any, is East-West, with the Baltic Countries and South East Europe behind the Europe average. However, the overall picture shows that EMI is now a pan-European phenomenon with approximately half of all HEIs offering EMI."

"欧洲高等教育中EMI的南北差距似乎已经消失。如果有分歧，那是东西方的分歧，波罗的海国家和东南欧地区落后于欧洲平均水平。然而，整体情况显示，EMI现在已成为泛欧洲现象，约有一半的高等教育机构提供EMI。"

- Wingrove et al., 2025

Conclusions and Implications

结论与影响

Theoretical & Methodological Implications:

理论与方法论影响：

  • The study applied a measurement validity framework to EMI, providing a more rigorous methodology for future research.
  • Findings suggest that EMI typologies should distinguish between undergraduate and postgraduate EMI.
  • The study highlights the need for standardization in EMI measurement to enable accurate comparisons.
  • 该研究应用了测量有效性框架于EMI，为未来研究提供了更严格的方法论。
  • 研究结果表明，EMI分类应该区分本科和研究生EMI。
  • 研究强调了EMI测量标准化的必要性，以实现准确比较。

Policy Implications:

政策影响：

  • The rapid growth of EMI requires policies ensuring sustainable, effective, and ethical applications.
  • Institutions must balance internationalization benefits against risks like domain loss in local languages.
  • Policymakers should ensure EMI practices respect linguistic diversity and provide equitable access to education.
  • EMI的快速增长需要确保可持续、有效和道德应用的政策。
  • 机构必须权衡国际化利益与本地语言领域丧失等风险。
  • 政策制定者应确保EMI实践尊重语言多样性，提供公平的教育机会。

"The rapid growth of EMI highlights the need for policies that ensure sustainable, effective, and ethical applications of EMI. While EMI has proven a powerful tool for internationalization and academic collaboration, its rapid growth raises ethical concerns, including the risk of domain loss in local languages and marginalizing non-English speaking staff and students."

"EMI的快速增长突显了需要确保EMI可持续、有效和道德应用的政策。虽然EMI已被证明是国际化和学术合作的强大工具，但其快速增长引发了道德问题，包括本地语言领域丧失的风险以及边缘化非英语母语的教职员工和学生。"

- Wingrove et al., 2025
