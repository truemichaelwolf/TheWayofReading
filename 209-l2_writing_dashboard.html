About the Author

Dr. Peter Crosthwaite is an Associate Professor in the School of Languages and Cultures at the University of Queensland, Australia. His research expertise includes corpus linguistics, second language writing, CALL, and English for Academic Purposes. He serves as editor-in-chief of the Australian Review of Applied Linguistics and is on editorial boards of leading Q1 journals.

关于作者

Peter Crosthwaite博士是澳大利亚昆士兰大学语言与文化学院副教授。他的研究专长包括语料库语言学、二语写作、计算机辅助语言学习和学术英语。他担任《澳大利亚应用语言学评论》主编，并在多个Q1期刊编委会任职。
