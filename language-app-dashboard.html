Study Overview

研究概述

This dashboard visualizes findings from a study examining the acceptance and use of language learning apps among 297 young adolescents (aged 12-13) in a self-directed context using the Unified Theory of Acceptance and Use of Technology (UTAUT).

"App-based language learning has gained attention for its potential to promote L2 learning beyond the classroom. However, few learners use L2 learning apps long enough to fully benefit from this learning method." (Hwang, 2025, p.1)

The study investigated what factors influence young adolescents' behavioral intention to use language learning apps outside of class and what influences their actual usage behavior.

此仪表板可视化了一项研究的发现，该研究使用技术接受与使用统一理论(UTAUT)考察了297名青少年(12-13岁)在自主学习环境中接受和使用语言学习应用程序的情况。

"基于应用程序的语言学习因其促进课堂外第二语言学习的潜力而受到关注。然而，很少有学习者使用第二语言学习应用程序的时间足够长，以充分受益于这种学习方法。" (黄炫彬，2025，第1页)

该研究调查了哪些因素影响青少年在课外使用语言学习应用程序的行为意向，以及哪些因素影响其实际使用行为。

Participant Demographics

参与者人口统计

297

The participants were first-grade middle school students in South Korea during a "free semester," which aims to foster well-rounded personal development by relieving academic stress and encouraging students to explore their interests.

None of the participants had prior experience with the focal technology, Mango Languages, before the study.

研究前，所有参与者都没有使用过研究中的目标技术Mango Languages的经验。

UTAUT Model Explained

UTAUT模型解释

The Unified Theory of Acceptance and Use of Technology (UTAUT) provides a meta-theoretical framework for understanding how individuals accept and use educational technologies. The table below explains each component:

UTAUT Variable Definition
Performance Expectancy (PE) The extent to which individuals find a technology useful for their performance (e.g., learning)
Effort Expectancy (EE) The extent to which individuals find a technology easy to use
Social Influence (SI) The extent to which individuals feel supported by socially important others (e.g., teachers, parents, peers) in their use of a technology
Facilitating Conditions (FC) The extent to which individuals evaluate their surrounding environments (e.g., devices, learning spaces) as conducive to using a technology
Behavioral Intention (BI) The extent to which individuals are inclined to accept a technology for use
Usage Behavior The extent to which individuals persistently use a technology
"The UTAUT predicts that individuals who perceive an educational technology as useful and convenient, when leveraged with social support and favorable conditions for its use, are more likely to develop a stronger intention to use it (i.e., acceptance). This heightened technology acceptance, in turn, leads to greater actual usage (i.e., persistence)." (Hwang, 2025, p.2)

技术接受与使用统一理论(UTAUT)提供了一个元理论框架，用于理解个人如何接受和使用教育技术。下表解释了每个组成部分：

UTAUT变量 定义
性能期望 (PE) 个人认为技术对其表现（例如学习）有用的程度
努力期望 (EE) 个人认为技术易于使用的程度
社会影响 (SI) 个人在使用技术时感受到社会重要他人（如教师、父母、同伴）支持的程度
促进条件 (FC) 个人评估其周围环境（如设备、学习空间）有利于使用技术的程度
行为意向 (BI) 个人倾向于接受使用技术的程度
使用行为 个人持续使用技术的程度
"UTAUT预测，当个人认为教育技术有用且方便，并且得到社会支持和有利的使用条件时，他们更有可能形成更强的使用意向（即接受）。这种更高的技术接受度又会导致更多的实际使用（即坚持）。"（黄炫彬，2025年，第2页）

Key Findings

主要发现

1
Perceived usefulness is critical for app acceptance

Performance expectancy (the extent to which students found the app useful for learning) was the strongest predictor of their behavioral intention to use the app.

2
Social support matters for young learners

Even though social influence scores were generally low (M = 2.77), social influence significantly predicted behavioral intention, suggesting that support from teachers, parents, and peers affects young adolescents' acceptance of language learning apps.

3
Favorable environments directly impact app usage

Facilitating conditions (access to devices, internet, comfortable learning spaces) directly influenced actual app usage behavior, showing greater impact than behavioral intention.

4
Ease of use was not significant

Effort expectancy did not significantly predict behavioral intention, possibly because the focal app was already highly user-friendly, so students didn't perceive it as presenting substantial challenges.

5
Few students use apps long enough to benefit

Only a small group of learners use apps long enough to fully benefit from this learning method. About 24% did not complete even one lesson during the 3-month period.

1
感知有用性对应用接受度至关重要

性能期望（学生认为应用对学习有用的程度）是其使用应用的行为意向的最强预测因素。

2
社会支持对年轻学习者很重要

尽管社会影响得分普遍较低（M = 2.77），但社会影响显著预测行为意向，表明来自教师、父母和同伴的支持会影响青少年对语言学习应用的接受度。

3
有利的环境直接影响应用使用

促进条件（获取设备、互联网、舒适的学习空间）直接影响实际应用使用行为，显示比行为意向更大的影响。

4
易用性影响不显著

努力期望对行为意向没有显著预测作用，可能是因为目标应用已经非常用户友好，所以学生不认为它存在实质性挑战。

5
很少有学生使用应用足够长时间以获益

只有少数学习者使用应用的时间足够长，能够充分受益于这种学习方法。约24%的学生在3个月期间甚至没有完成一节课。

Pedagogical Implications

教学启示

Based on the study's findings, here are key implications for enhancing young adolescents' app-based language learning in self-study settings:

1
Emphasize app usefulness

Teachers should raise students' awareness of how apps can benefit their L2 learning by explicitly demonstrating app features and providing hands-on tasks that involve app usage.

"One effective approach could be explicitly demonstrating app features and providing hands-on tasks that involve app usage. For example, with Mango Languages, teachers can have students practice using the built-in flashcard function to review key vocabulary and acoustic visualization which provides real-time feedback on their pronunciation performance." (Hwang, 2025, p.12)
2
Provide consistent social encouragement

Given the significant impact of social influence, teachers should integrate frequent reminders or casual discussions about apps into their everyday teaching practices.

"This support does not have to be formal or time-intensive interventions. For example, teachers simply can integrate frequent reminders or casual discussions about apps into their everyday teaching practices, reinforcing the idea that necessary support is always available to them, even when learning occurs beyond the classroom." (Hwang, 2025, p.12)
3
Use reflective journaling

Implement reflective journal writing to encourage students to evaluate their app usage and identify areas for improvement.

"Another effective method involves students keeping a diary of their app-based language learning experiences. This reflective journaling encourages students to evaluate their app usage and identify areas for improvement, while also enabling teachers to provide personalized feedback and targeted support." (Hwang, 2025, p.12)
4
Ensure access to necessary resources

Teachers should ensure that students have access to the necessary resources and technical support beyond the classroom, particularly for students from diverse socioeconomic backgrounds.

"The presence of conducive environments (e.g., access to smart devices, stable Internet connectivity, comfortable learning spaces) directly influenced out-of-class app usage, showing greater impacts than technology acceptance." (Hwang, 2025, p.12)

基于研究发现，以下是提高青少年在自主学习环境中基于应用程序语言学习的关键启示：

1
强调应用的有用性

教师应通过明确展示应用功能并提供涉及应用使用的实践任务，提高学生对应用如何促进第二语言学习的认识。

"一种有效的方法可以是明确展示应用功能并提供涉及应用使用的实践任务。例如，使用Mango Languages时，教师可以让学生练习使用内置的闪卡功能复习关键词汇和声音可视化，后者提供关于其发音表现的实时反馈。"（黄炫彬，2025年，第12页）
2
提供持续的社会鼓励

鉴于社会影响的显著作用，教师应将频繁的提醒或关于应用的日常讨论融入日常教学实践中。

"这种支持不必是正式或耗时的干预。例如，教师可以简单地将有关应用的频繁提醒或随意讨论融入日常教学实践中，强化即使在课堂外学习时，必要的支持也始终可用的理念。"（黄炫彬，2025年，第12页）
3
使用反思日记

实施反思性日记写作，鼓励学生评估自己的应用使用情况并确定需要改进的领域。

"另一种有效的方法涉及让学生记录基于应用程序的语言学习经验的日记。这种反思性日记写作鼓励学生评估自己的应用使用情况并确定需要改进的领域，同时也使教师能够提供个性化反馈和有针对性的支持。"（黄炫彬，2025年，第12页）
4
确保获取必要资源

教师应确保学生能够在课堂外获得必要的资源和技术支持，特别是来自不同社会经济背景的学生。

"有利环境（如获取智能设备、稳定的互联网连接、舒适的学习空间）的存在直接影响课外应用使用，表现出比技术接受度更大的影响。"（黄炫彬，2025年，第12页）
