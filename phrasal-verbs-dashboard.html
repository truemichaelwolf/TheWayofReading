Contextual Learning of Phrasal Verbs Research Dashboard 短语动词语境学习研究数据面板

Key Findings 主要发现

  • Definitions after reading resulted in better learning outcomes
  • Typographic enhancement improved learning
  • Immediate recall: 60.5% accuracy
  • Delayed recall: 27.5% accuracy
  • 阅读后提供定义带来更好的学习效果
  • 排版强调改善了学习效果
  • 即时回忆：60.5%的准确率
  • 延迟回忆：27.5%的准确率
"The results showed a clear advantage of learning with definitions and presenting definitions after reading." - p.20

Methodology 研究方法

Participants & Materials

  • 85 high school students (16-18 years)
  • 42 figurative phrasal verbs
  • 3 repetitions per text
  • Both immediate and delayed tests

Treatment Groups

Condition Definition Placement Enhancement
1 Before text Yes
2 Before text No
3 After text Yes
4 After text No
5 None Yes
6 None No

参与者与材料

  • 85名高中生 (16-18岁)
  • 42个比喻性短语动词
  • 每个文本重复3次
  • 即时和延迟测试

实验组别

条件 定义位置 排版强调
1 文本之前 是
2 文本之前 否
3 文本之后 是
4 文本之后 否
5 无 是
6 无 否
"The current study involved a 2 × 3 within-participant design. The independent variables were typographic enhancement with two levels (enhanced, unenhanced), and definition placement with three levels (before text, after text, no definition)." - p.9

Research Implications 研究启示

  1. Definitions should be provided after contextual exposure
  2. Use typographic enhancement for better retention
  3. Allow learners to make contextual inferences first
  4. Support contextual learning with explicit instruction
  1. 应在语境接触后提供定义
  2. 使用排版强调以提高记忆效果
  3. 先让学习者进行语境推断
  4. 用明确的指导支持语境学习
"Our advice to teachers, publishers, and material developers is not to take inference opportunities away from L2 learners for fear of possible erroneous contextual inferences." - p.20
