GO (a)round and V vs. GO (a)round Ving

A Multivariate Distinctive Collexeme Analysis

This interactive dashboard visualizes the findings from the research paper on multivariate distinctive collexeme analysis of the GO (a)round and V vs. GO (a)round Ving constructions by Kim Ebensgaard Jensen and Stefan Th. Gries (2025).

"Distinctive collexeme analysis has proven very useful in identifying distinctive patterns of lexemic attraction among multiple constructions. However, if construction grammar is to take seriously the usage-based tenet that context is a crucial component of the language system, multivariate methods are required."

The study applies a novel multivariate collexeme analysis based on association rules to analyze two seemingly similar constructions:

  • GO (a)round and V: e.g., "He's going around and spying on us."
  • GO (a)round Ving: e.g., "Well I don't go around assaulting people."

8 Features Analyzed

The researchers analyzed these constructions using 8 different feature dimensions:

Collexeme

Lexemes in the V and Ving positions (214 lexemes total)

Colligation

Grammatical categories co-occurring with the constructions (e.g., simple present, progressive aspect)

Modality

Types of modality: epistemic, deontic, or none

Polarity

Negative polarity (with negator) or affirmative polarity (without negator)

Semantic Prosody

Emergent semantic categories among collexemes (120 prosodies total)

Discourse Prosody

Attitudinal patterns: positive, negative, or neutral

Register

Registers from COCA: academic, fiction, magazines, movies, news, spoken, TV

Speech Act

Function of utterances: statements, questions, directives, commissives

Key Findings

GO (a)round and V

  • Associated with neutral discourse prosody
  • Only appears with deontic modality in rules
  • Strongly associated with spoken register
  • Only appears with affirmative polarity (never with negation)
  • Associated with controlled future tense, especially with affirmative polarity
  • No distinctive collexemes or semantic prosodies were identified due to low frequency

GO (a)round Ving

  • Preferred by specific verbs: do, kill, look, pretend, talk, think
  • Preferred by verbs with 'unpleasant' semantic prosodies (ACCUSATION, DESTRUCTION, IMMODESTY, THREAT, VIOLENCE)
  • Strong preference for negative discourse prosody (20 rules vs. only 4 for positive)
  • Associated with directives and commissives speech acts (never with positive discourse prosody)
  • Often involves do-insertion and modalized VP
  • Only associates with deontic modality (not epistemic)
  • Negative contexts appear more in fictional representations of spoken language (fiction, movies, TV) than actual spoken language
"Our analysis suggests that the concept of distinctiveness must necessarily be expanded to include a construction's broader context of usage. This is in line with Kemmer and Barlow's (2000) usage-based tenet that context plays a crucial role in the operation of the language system."

多变量区别性搭配词分析

这个交互式仪表板可视化了Kim Ebensgaard Jensen和Stefan Th. Gries（2025年）关于GO (a)round and V与GO (a)round Ving结构的多变量区别性搭配词分析研究论文的发现。

"区别性搭配词分析在识别多个结构之间的词汇吸引模式方面被证明非常有用。然而，如果构式语法要认真对待基于使用的原则，即语境是语言系统的关键组成部分，则需要多变量方法。"

该研究应用了一种基于关联规则的新型多变量搭配词分析方法，分析了两种看似相似的结构：

  • GO (a)round and V：例如，"He's going around and spying on us."（他正在到处监视我们。）
  • GO (a)round Ving：例如，"Well I don't go around assaulting people."（我不会到处攻击人。）

分析的8个特征

研究人员使用8个不同的特征维度分析了这些结构：

搭配词（Collexeme）

V和Ving位置上的词汇（共214个词汇）

词类搭配（Colligation）

与结构共现的语法类别（如：一般现在时，进行时态）

情态（Modality）

情态类型：认识情态、道义情态或无

极性（Polarity）

否定极性（有否定词）或肯定极性（无否定词）

语义韵律（Semantic Prosody）

搭配词中的语义类别（共120种韵律）

话语韵律（Discourse Prosody）

态度模式：积极、消极或中性

语域（Register）

COCA中的语域：学术、小说、杂志、电影、新闻、口语、电视

言语行为（Speech Act）

话语功能：陈述、提问、指令、承诺

主要发现

GO (a)round and V

  • 与中性话语韵律相关
  • 在规则中仅出现道义情态
  • 与口语语域强相关
  • 仅出现肯定极性（从不与否定词一起使用）
  • 与受控未来时态相关，尤其是在肯定极性条件下
  • 由于频率较低，未识别出明显的搭配词或语义韵律

GO (a)round Ving

  • 特定动词偏好：do, kill, look, pretend, talk, think
  • 偏好带有"不愉快"语义韵律的动词（指责、破坏、不适当、威胁、暴力）
  • 强烈偏好负面话语韵律（20条规则 vs. 仅4条积极规则）
  • 与指令和承诺言语行为相关（从不与积极话语韵律一起出现）
  • 经常涉及do插入和情态化动词短语
  • 仅与道义情态相关（非认识情态）
  • 负面语境在口语的虚构表现（小说、电影、电视）中出现得比实际口语中更多
"我们的分析表明，区别性的概念必须扩展到包括结构更广泛的使用语境。这与Kemmer和Barlow（2000）基于使用的原则一致，即语境在语言系统的运作中起着关键作用。"

Research Implications

Theoretical Implications

The concept of distinctiveness must be expanded to include a construction's broader context of usage, supporting Kemmer and Barlow's (2000) tenet that context plays a crucial role in language systems.

Methodological Contribution

Multivariate collexeme analysis provides richer descriptions of constructions than traditional collostructional analyses by identifying complex configurations of features.

Understanding Constructions

Constructions should be viewed as multidimensional entities with both internal and external properties, reintroducing Fillmore's (1988) distinction.

Functional Difference

The analysis reveals that GO (a)round Ving tends to express disapproval of unpleasant behaviors, while GO (a)round and V has more neutral discourse functions.

"Constructions could be described in terms of what Herbst (2018) calls collo-profiles — representations of 'collo-items' occurring with constructions. Essentially, our findings in this study constitute a step towards building what we can call distinctive collo-profiles pertaining to the two constructions."
理论意义

区别性的概念必须扩展到包括结构更广泛的使用语境，支持Kemmer和Barlow（2000）的原则，即语境在语言系统中起着关键作用。

方法论贡献

多变量搭配词分析通过识别复杂的特征配置，提供了比传统搭配结构分析更丰富的结构描述。

理解构式

构式应被视为具有内部和外部属性的多维实体，重新引入Fillmore（1988）的区分。

功能差异

分析表明，GO (a)round Ving倾向于表达对不愉快行为的不认同，而GO (a)round and V具有更中性的话语功能。

"构式可以用Herbst（2018）所称的搭配概况（collo-profiles）来描述——表示与构式一起出现的'搭配项目'。本质上，我们在这项研究中的发现构成了构建我们可以称之为与这两种构式相关的独特搭配概况的一步。"
