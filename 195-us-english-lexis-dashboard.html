Lexical Creation Processes

US English has developed new words through various processes:

  • Compounding: combining existing words (backwoods, cold-snap)
  • Blending: combining parts of words (motel, sitcom)
  • Semantic shift: changing meaning of existing words (corn, frontier)
  • Conversion: changing word class (Google as verb)
  • Clipping: shortening words (ad, math)
"The creation and use of compound words, especially those with a hyphen, has increased significantly in the past century in US English." - Liu & Wu (2025)

Ethnic Variations

US English has distinctive ethnic variations, including African American, Latino, and Native American varieties.

British vs. American English

US English differs from British English in both orthography and vocabulary.

Orthographic Differences:

  • -re → -er (center vs. centre)
  • -our → -or (color vs. colour)
  • -ise → -ize (realize vs. realise)

词汇创造过程

美国英语通过各种过程发展出新词：

  • 合成：组合现有词汇 (backwoods, cold-snap)
  • 混成：组合词的部分 (motel, sitcom)
  • 语义转移：改变现有词的含义 (corn, frontier)
  • 转换：改变词性 (Google作为动词)
  • 截短：缩短词 (ad, math)
"在过去的一个世纪里，美国英语中复合词的创造和使用，尤其是那些带连字符的词，已显著增加。" - 刘 & 吴 (2025)
