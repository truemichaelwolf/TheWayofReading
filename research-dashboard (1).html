The Impact of Research Paradigms on Encoding and Decoding Difficulties of Applied Linguistics Articles

研究范式对应用语言学文章编码和解码难度的影响

Jiayi Wu, Hongzhong Chen & Lang Chen
Humanities and Social Sciences Communications (2025)
人文社会科学通讯 (2025)

Background & Research Gap

研究背景与研究缺口

Academic writing has evolved in response to increasing publication pressure in the current "attention economy" of academia. Previous studies have examined either disciplinary variations or treated disciplines as homogeneous categories, overlooking potential differences within disciplines based on research paradigms.

"Little attention has been given to the potential interaction between the research paradigm (quantitative vs. qualitative) and the diachronic change within a specific discipline."

Research Questions

  1. Is there a significant difference in Information Entropy (IE) between Applied Linguistics articles from 1981-1985 vs. 2011-2015?
  2. Do qualitative and quantitative articles show the same changing pattern in IE?
  3. Is there a significant difference in Mean Dependency Distance (MDD) between articles from these two periods?
  4. Do qualitative and quantitative articles show the same changing pattern in MDD?

学术写作随着当前学术界"注意力经济"中日益增长的出版压力而发展。以往的研究要么关注学科差异，要么将学科视为同质类别，忽视了基于研究范式的学科内潜在差异。

"很少有人关注研究范式（定量与定性）与特定学科内历时变化之间的潜在相互作用。"

研究问题

  1. 1981-1985年与2011-2015年的应用语言学文章在信息熵(IE)方面是否存在显著差异？
  2. 定性和定量文章在IE方面是否表现出相同的变化模式？
  3. 这两个时期的文章在平均依存距离(MDD)方面是否存在显著差异？
  4. 定性和定量文章在MDD方面是否表现出相同的变化模式？

Methodology

研究方法

Corpus Design

  • 160 full-length empirical research articles in Applied Linguistics
  • From 4 prestigious journals: Applied Linguistics, Modern Language Journal, TESOL Quarterly, and Language Learning
  • Stratified by time period (1981-1985 vs. 2011-2015) and research paradigm (qualitative vs. quantitative)
  • 40 articles in each of the 4 categories

语料库设计

  • 160篇应用语言学领域的完整实证研究文章
  • 来源于4本著名期刊：Applied Linguistics, Modern Language Journal, TESOL Quarterly和Language Learning
  • 按时间段（1981-1985年vs. 2011-2015年）和研究范式（定性vs.定量）分层
  • 4个类别各40篇文章

Cognitive Difficulty Measures

Information Entropy (IE): Measures cognitive encoding difficulty (challenges for writers) based on the unpredictability of word occurrences.

Mean Dependency Distance (MDD): Measures cognitive decoding difficulty (challenges for readers) based on the average syntactic distance between dependency-related words.

认知难度测量

信息熵 (IE)：基于词语出现的不可预测性，测量认知编码难度（作者面临的挑战）。

平均依存距离 (MDD)：基于依存关系词语之间的平均句法距离，测量认知解码难度（读者面临的挑战）。

Analysis Methods

  • Python scripts with spaCy library for natural language processing
  • Transformer-based language model for dependency parsing
  • Two-way multivariate analysis of variance (MANOVA)
  • Post-hoc pairwise comparisons with Bonferroni corrections

分析方法

  • 使用spaCy库的Python脚本进行自然语言处理
  • 基于Transformer的语言模型用于依存关系解析
  • 双因素多元方差分析 (MANOVA)
  • 采用Bonferroni校正的事后两两比较

Key Findings

主要发现

Information Entropy (Cognitive Encoding Difficulty)

发现1: 只有定量文章的平均依存距离（MDD）随时间显著增加。

发现2: 在1981-1985年期间，定性文章的MDD高于定量文章。

发现3: 在2011-2015年期间，这一模式发生逆转，定量文章的MDD高于定性文章。

发现4: 时间和研究范式对MDD有显著的交互作用。

Combined Analysis of Encoding and Decoding Difficulties

Finding 1: Both qualitative and quantitative articles have become more difficult to encode (write) over time.

Finding 2: Quantitative articles have also become more difficult to decode (read) over time, while qualitative articles showed minimal change in this dimension.

Finding 3: The interaction between research paradigm and time revealed distinctive patterns of cognitive difficulty, suggesting a paradigm shift in how knowledge is constructed and presented in different research traditions.

编码和解码难度综合分析

发现1: 定性和定量文章随着时间的推移都变得更难编码（写作）。

发现2: 定量文章随着时间的推移也变得更难解码（阅读），而定性文章在这一维度上变化很小。

发现3: 研究范式与时间的交互揭示了认知难度的独特模式，表明不同研究传统中知识构建和呈现方式存在范式转变。

Discussion & Significance

讨论与意义

Epistemological Differences

The distinctive patterns in cognitive complexity between qualitative and quantitative research articles reflect their underlying epistemological differences:

  • Quantitative paradigm (rooted in positivist epistemology):
    • Seeks to establish general laws of causality
    • Research conducted in controlled conditions
    • More predictable structure and content
    • Standard formats for reporting findings
  • Qualitative paradigm (grounded in constructivist epistemology):
    • Focuses on context-specific understanding
    • Research conducted in natural settings
    • Richer descriptions of research contexts
    • More nuanced interpretation of findings
    • More complex and varied linguistic expressions
"The significant paradigmatic differences indicate that research paradigm needs to be taken into consideration for a better understanding of the cognitive complexity in RAs."

认识论差异

定性和定量研究文章之间认知复杂性的不同模式反映了它们潜在的认识论差异：

  • 定量范式（根植于实证主义认识论）：
    • 寻求建立普遍因果律则
    • 在受控条件下进行研究
    • 结构和内容更可预测
    • 使用标准格式报告研究发现
  • 定性范式（基于建构主义认识论）：
    • 关注特定情境下的理解
    • 在自然环境中进行研究
    • 对研究背景有更丰富的描述
    • 对研究发现有更细致的解释
    • 使用更复杂多样的语言表达
"显著的范式差异表明，需要考虑研究范式以更好地理解研究文章中的认知复杂性。"

Publication Pressure & Promotional Language

The increasing cognitive difficulties in academic articles may be linked to changes in how knowledge is communicated in the modern academic environment:

  • Rising Information Entropy (IE):
    • Accumulative nature of academic knowledge
    • Increasing volume of publications
    • Cross-disciplinary integration in Applied Linguistics
  • Changes in Mean Dependency Distance (MDD):
    • Rise in promotional language to enhance visibility
    • Competition for attention in quantitative research
    • Use of attitude markers and boosters
"While promotional language may enhance the visibility of scientific discoveries, its increasing use may also contribute to higher cognitive decoding difficulty of RAs."

出版压力与宣传性语言

学术文章中认知难度的增加可能与现代学术环境中知识传播方式的变化有关：

  • 信息熵(IE)的上升：
    • 学术知识的累积性质
    • 出版物数量的增加
    • 应用语言学中的跨学科整合
  • 平均依存距离(MDD)的变化：
    • 宣传性语言的增加以提高可见度
    • 定量研究中对关注度的竞争
    • 态度标记和强化语的使用
"尽管宣传性语言可能提高科学发现的可见性，但其日益增加的使用也可能导致研究文章的认知解码难度提高。"

Implications & Contributions

  • Theoretical Implications: Reveals the importance of research paradigms in shaping cognitive complexity within a discipline.
  • Research Methodology: Demonstrates the value of examining both encoding and decoding difficulties for a complete understanding of text complexity.
  • Academic Writing Instruction: Informs how different research traditions may require different approaches to writing instruction.
  • Editorial Practices: Suggests implications for journal editorial policies and review practices to ensure accessibility while maintaining informativeness.
  • Future Research: Calls for more nuanced investigations of the relationships between promotional language and cognitive difficulty in academic writing.
"This study contributes to existing literature by revealing the nuanced patterns in academic writing within a specific discipline, thereby deepening our understanding of disciplinary writing."

启示与贡献

  • 理论意义： 揭示了研究范式在塑造学科内认知复杂性方面的重要性。
  • 研究方法： 展示了同时研究编码和解码难度对全面理解文本复杂性的价值。
  • 学术写作教学： 为不同研究传统可能需要不同的写作教学方法提供了信息。
  • 编辑实践： 对期刊编辑政策和审阅实践提出建议，以确保可及性的同时保持信息量。
  • 未来研究： 呼吁对学术写作中宣传性语言与认知难度之间的关系进行更细致的研究。
"本研究通过揭示特定学科内学术写作的细微模式，对现有文献做出了贡献，从而加深了我们对学科写作的理解。"

Finding 1: Both qualitative and quantitative articles showed significant increases in Information Entropy (IE) over time.

Finding 2: Qualitative articles consistently exhibited higher IE than quantitative articles in both time periods.

Finding 3: The time factor had a larger effect (η² = 0.334) than the paradigm factor (η² = 0.105) on IE.

信息熵（认知编码难度）

发现1: 定性和定量文章的信息熵(IE)随时间的推移都显著增加。

发现2: 在两个时间段内，定性文章的IE始终高于定量文章。

发现3: 时间因素(η² = 0.334)对IE的影响大于范式因素(η² = 0.105)。

Mean Dependency Distance (Cognitive Decoding Difficulty)

Finding 1: Only quantitative articles showed a significant increase in Mean Dependency Distance (MDD) over time.

Finding 2: In the 1981-1985 period, qualitative articles had higher MDD than quantitative articles.

Finding 3: In the 2011-2015 period, this pattern reversed, with quantitative articles showing higher MDD than qualitative articles.

Finding 4: There was a significant interaction effect between time and research paradigm on MDD.
